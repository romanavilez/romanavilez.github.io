What is ChildWay

A mobile child safety app that accompanies your children everywhere they go, whenever you can't. Unlike traditional tracking apps that only give you a target's location, ChildWay uses anomaly detection and location tracking to alert you when your child is in an unsafe situation, such as being in an unfamiliar location or in a location for an unusually long time. With the addition of map navigation, messaging, and SOS alerts, your child is not only kept safe, but also gives them autonomy to explore the world around them with confidence. ChildWay strikes the perfect balance between autonomy and safety, giving you peace of mind while allowing your child to grow and learn independently.

Why introduce ChildWay to your family

- Helps you quickly detect potentially dangerous situations

- Provides real-time alerts when unusual behavior occurs

- Reduces the need for constant manual monitoring

- Gives you peace of mind when your children are away

- Offers a centralized app to manage your child's safety and communication needs

How does ChildWay work

1) Create a parent account

2) Invite or connect your child's device by username or QR code

3) Set up your child's profile by customizing boundary controls and alert preferences

4) Allow the app to learn routines to establish a baseline for normal behavior and patterns

5) Start receiving real-time updates when anomalies are detected, boundaries are crossed, and SOS alerts are sent
